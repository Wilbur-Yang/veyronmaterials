FAQ

Common questions from B2B buyers.

Answers are kept practical for first-stage inquiry discussions. Final specifications are confirmed case by case.

What products do you supply?

We support PBAT modified material, biodegradable blown film, biodegradable mulch film, mailer bags, garbage bags, flexible packaging bags and compostable takeout boxes.

Can I request samples?

Samples can be discussed for qualified B2B inquiries. Availability depends on product type, current stock and project requirements.

What is your MOQ?

MOQ depends on product type, size, material, printing, packaging and order plan.

Can you customise size, thickness, colour or packaging?

Yes. Custom size, thickness, color, printing and packaging can be discussed according to the product and order plan.

Can you review certification information?

Australian certification information can be reviewed during qualified B2B discussions. Exact certificate name, number and covered product scope should be confirmed before an order.

Do you support private label or OEM packaging?

Private label and OEM packaging can be reviewed based on product type, artwork, order quantity and packaging requirements.

Which customers are suitable for cooperation?

Packaging brands, distributors, wholesalers, importers, e-commerce packaging buyers and eco-packaging companies are suitable for discussion.
