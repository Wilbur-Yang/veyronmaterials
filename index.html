About Veyron Materials

Overseas communication brand for biodegradable products and PBAT material solutions.

Veyron Materials is the overseas market communication brand of Jiangsu Weiyuan New Material Technology Co., Ltd., focusing on biodegradable plastic products, PBAT modified materials and flexible packaging solutions.

We work with manufacturing and supply chain partners in China to support biodegradable packaging products and PBAT-based material solutions for overseas B2B clients.

Veyron Materials has obtained Australian certification, supporting supply discussions for biodegradable packaging products and PBAT modified materials in the Australian market.

FAQ

Common questions from B2B buyers.

Answers are kept practical for first-stage inquiry discussions. Final specifications are confirmed case by case.

What products do you supply?

We supply biodegradable mailer bags, garbage bags, flexible packaging bags, biodegradable mulch film, compostable takeout boxes and PBAT modified material.

Can I request samples?

Samples can be discussed for qualified B2B inquiries. Availability depends on product type and current stock.

What is your MOQ?

MOQ depends on product type, size, material, printing and packaging requirements.

Can you customise size, thickness, colour or packaging?

Yes. Custom size, thickness, color, printing and packaging can be discussed according to the product and order plan.

Have you obtained Australian certification?

Yes. Veyron Materials has obtained Australian certification for relevant B2B supply discussions.

Do you support private label or OEM packaging?

Private label and OEM packaging can be reviewed based on product type, artwork, order quantity and packaging requirements.

Which customers are suitable for cooperation?

Packaging brands, distributors, wholesalers, e-commerce packaging buyers and eco-packaging companies are suitable for discussion.
