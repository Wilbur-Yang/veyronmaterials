# Marketing

## Brand

Veyron Materials

## Business Positioning

Overseas B2B communication brand for biodegradable packaging products and PBAT material solutions.

## Target Buyers

- Packaging brands.
- Distributors.
- Wholesalers.
- E-commerce packaging buyers.
- Eco-packaging companies.
- Overseas buyers discussing Australian and international supply.

## Product Categories

- PBAT modified material / masterbatch.
- Biodegradable blown film.
- Biodegradable mulch film.
- Biodegradable mailer bags.
- Biodegradable garbage bags.
- Flexible packaging bags.
- Compostable takeout boxes.

## Core Buyer Needs

- Product specification clarity.
- Sample availability.
- MOQ flexibility.
- Custom size, thickness, color, packaging, and printing.
- Certification and compliance confidence.
- Reliable inquiry and quote process.

## Current Website Message

Veyron Materials supports overseas B2B buyers with biodegradable packaging and PBAT material inquiries, including samples, customization, flexible MOQ, and Australian certification positioning.

## Trust Signals To Strengthen

- Exact Australian certification name.
- Certificate number or document.
- Covered product scope.
- Whether certificate image can be published.
- Real product photos or factory/process photos if available.
- Clear business contact path beyond `mailto:`.

## Marketing Risks

- Avoid unsupported certification claims.
- Avoid vague sustainability wording.
- Avoid implying all products have the same certification unless verified.
- Avoid relying only on generated images if launch credibility requires real proof.

## Copy Direction

Tone should be:

- Practical.
- Procurement-friendly.
- Specific.
- B2B-focused.
- Trustworthy.

Avoid:

- Overly broad slogans.
- Decorative copy without buyer value.
- Claims that cannot be backed by documents.
